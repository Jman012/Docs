Understanding a Tox IDÂ¶

A Tox ID you distribute is a public key, nospam value, and checksum; in hexadecimal format. This results in 32 characters + 8 characters + 4 characters, for 48.

So something like

56A1ADE4B65B86BCD51CC73E2CD4E542179F47959FE3E0E21B4B0ACDADE5185520B3E6FC5D64

Can be broken down to

Public Key:[56A1ADE4B65B86BCD51CC73E2CD4E542179F47959FE3E0E21B4B0ACDADE51855] nospam:[20B3E6FC] checksum:[5D64]
